About System


The Online Food Booking System is a web-based application designed to simplify the process of ordering and managing food services through an online platform. The system enables customers to browse menus, place food orders, make payments, and track their order status conveniently from their computers or mobile devices. It bridges the gap between customers and restaurants by providing a digital interface that ensures speed, accuracy, and efficiency in food ordering and delivery services. The system has been developed with the primary objective of minimizing manual operations and enhancing the customer experience by automating the food ordering process. Through this platform, users can register, log in, view available restaurants and menus, add items to their cart, confirm orders, and make payments online. The restaurant administrators can manage menu items, update prices, view orders in real time, and track order completion.


The Online Food Booking System also helps restaurant owners manage customer data, order history, and feedback efficiently. The integration of modern technologies ensures secure transactions and easy accessibility, thereby reducing operational costs and improving service quality.This project aims to demonstrate the application of database management, web development, and software engineering concepts in a real-world scenario. It has been designed with a user-friendly interface and modular architecture to ensure scalability, maintainability, and ease of use


In the future, the system can be expanded to include additional features such as real-time delivery tracking using GPS, promotional offers, AI-based food recommendations, and integration with third-party delivery partners. The system ultimately seeks to enhance convenience for customers, streamline restaurant operations, and promote the digital transformation of the food service industry.


About Ordering


The ordering process is the core function of the Online Food Booking System, designed to make food selection and purchase simple, fast, and efficient for users. Through this system, customers can browse a variety of restaurants and menu items, view detailed descriptions and prices, and place their desired orders online without the need for physical visits or phone calls. Once logged in, a user can select a restaurant, choose preferred food items, specify quantities, and add them to a virtual shopping cart. After reviewing the cart, the customer can proceed to confirm the order and make payment using the available payment options such as online wallets, debit/credit cards, or cash on delivery. The system automatically records the order details in the database and sends a confirmation message to both the customer and the restaurant.


On the restaurant’s side, the order is received instantly through the admin panel, where staff can view, prepare, and update the order status (e.g., Pending, Preparing, Ready, or Delivered). Customers can also track their order progress in real time, ensuring transparency and better service experience. This automated ordering process reduces errors that occur in manual food ordering, saves time for both customers and restaurant staff, and enhances overall efficiency. It ensures accuracy in billing, maintains proper order history, and allows restaurants to analyze sales and customer preferences effectively.
